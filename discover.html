Discover

Why I chose University of Minnesota Duluth

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth for its location, the strong opportunities available in my career field, and to be part of the tennis program. At UMD I've been able to get involved in the community through clubs, activities, and networking with employers and faculty. The campus offers a supportive environment and excellent opportunities for growth in both academics and athletics.

What I like

I love Lake Superior — its beauty and the peaceful moments along the shore are special to me. I also enjoy attending sporting events and cheering on teams. My grandma lives in Virginia, Minnesota, so I’m fortunate to visit her often; family time is something I truly treasure.

Personal Experience

My time at the University of Minnesota Duluth has been full of unforgettable moments. I’ve explored Bentleyville with friends, witnessed the northern lights, and cheered on UMD’s hockey team. I've also participated in the Green Bandanna leadership program and had the opportunity to coach tennis at the Duluth Indoor Sports Center. Duluth has given me meaningful experiences both on and off campus, and I’m deeply grateful for the community I've found here.
